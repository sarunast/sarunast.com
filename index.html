About me

Originally I am from Lithuania. I was always interested in different kinds of media - creating, editing and sharing it. Usually in the summer I would come up with crazy ideas. And so it happened that one of them was to build a website (I think I was around 15 at that time). I got a simple book on how to build websites and slowly started learning. Funny enough that It was so interesting to me that I went to UK to study Web Development & Design. Until this day I am still working as web developer and still enjoying it.

What am I doing now ?

Currently I am living in Austria on the mountains and working at Wikitude. I mainly focus on Frontend. On a daily basis I have to deal with Node and Angular. At the moment I am interested in Reactive and Functional Programming and experimenting libraries like React, Redux and looking forward a lot to play with RxJs.

Besides learning and experimenting with new frameworks and technologies I enjoy hiking and taking pictures. If am going somewhere I will usually take photo camera with me. If you want to see my recent shots you can easily find them on flickr.
